Go to github.com and sign up. After you have signed up, click the account icon on the upper right hand side of the screen, and then select YOUR REPOSITORIES from the drop down menu. Click the green button labeled NEW. In the text box next to the owner name, type in a name for your repository. Click CREATE REPOSITORY. On the next page click UPLOAD AN EXISTING FILE. Choose the file you have created in VSCode to upload to Github. This is a start.
